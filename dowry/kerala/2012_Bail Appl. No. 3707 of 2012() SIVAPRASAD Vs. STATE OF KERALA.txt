                          O R D E R


     Petitioners   are   accused      Nos.1 to     4 in  Crime

No.423/2012 of Karunagappally Police Station. The offence

alleged against them is under Sec.498A r/w 34 IPC. First

accused is the husband. Others are the mother, father and

sister of the first accused. (The age of the 2nd petitioner

shown in the petition should be wrong). It is alleged that

petitioners subjected the complainant to physical and

mental cruelty demanding more money and gold as dowry.

It is stated that there is a petition pending before the

Family Court also.

     2.   Considering     all   the    aspects,   the following

directions are issued :-

          In the event of arrest, the petitioners shall be

released on bail on their executing a bond for Rs.20,000/-
