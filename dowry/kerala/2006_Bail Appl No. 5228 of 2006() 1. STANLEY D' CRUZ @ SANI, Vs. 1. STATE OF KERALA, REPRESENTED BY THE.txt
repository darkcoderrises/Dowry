                             O R D E R




             The petitioner is the first accused,  in crime No.


390/2006 of Alappuzha Town North Police Station, for the


offence punishable under Sections 498A and 323 IPC read


with Section 34 IPC.


      2.     The   allegation   is   that   the   petitioner   had


assaulted   his   wife   as   well   as   his   father-in-law,   and   also


demanded more dowry.   The accused Nos.2  to 4 had also


perpetuated cruelties, on her.


      3.     After   hearing   both   sides   and   considering   the


materials on record,   I direct the   petitioner to surrender


before the Investigating Officer, who is investigating crime


No. 390/2006 of Alappuzha Town North Police Station, on


31.8.2006, between 10.30 a.m and 11.00 a.m.


      4.     The   Investigating   Officer   shall   interrogate   the


petitioner and produce him before the Court below, as per


the law.     The petitioner may move an application for bail
