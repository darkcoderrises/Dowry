                           O R D E R

     The petitioner is the accused in Crime No.484/2014 of

Kayamkulam Police Station, Alappuzha District.         Offences

alleged are under sections 498A and 506(i) of IPC.

Apprehending arrest, this petition is filed for anticipatory

bail.

     2.     The petitioner is the husband of the complainant.

Marriage between the petitioner and the complainant was

solemnized on 22.06.2013.            It is alleged that the

complainant was subjected to physical and mental cruelty

and demanded more amount as dowry.

     3.   The learned counsel for the petitioner submits that

the allegations are not true.

     4.   Considering all the aspects the following directions

are issued:

accused shall be produced before the learned Magistrate.

       b.     The   petitioner  shall  make    himself

              available   for interrogation   by  the

              Investigating Officer and shall appear
