                              O R D E R




              The   petitioners   are   before   this   court     praying


for a pre-arrest order,   as they apprehend arrest in crime


No.431/2006   of   Anchal   Police   Station,     for   the   offence


punishable under Sections   120B, 323, 324 and 498A IPC


read with Section 34 IPC and also under Section 4 of the


Dowry Prohibition Act.


       2.     The   petitioners   1   and   3   are   advocates  and   the


first petitioner  is the son of the petitioners 2 and 3.   The


wife   of   the   first   petitioner   is   also   an   advocate,   who   was


practicing attached with  the office of the third petitioner,


who is a senior lawyer of that area.   The   petitioners had


produced the copy of the complaint,  which was addressed


to different authorities.


       2.     After   hearing   both   sides   and   considering   the


facts that are placed before me, I direct the petitioners to


surrender before the Court below and move  application(s)


B.A.5783/2006

                                    2


for bail.


        3.     The   learned   Magistrate   shall   hear   both   sides


and   allow   the   petitioners   to   be   on   bail,   imposing   such


conditions,     including   that   the   petitioners   shall   be


available   before   the   Investigating   Officer,     for   the


purpose of the completion of  investigation.


               The application is disposed of as above.
