                           O R D E R

     The petitioner is the 2nd accused in Crime No.158/2014

of Edacherry Police Station, Kozhikode District.       Offences

alleged are under sections 498A, 354 and 406 r/w 34 of IPC.

Apprehending arrest, this petition is filed for anticipatory

bail.

     2.    The complainant was married by the 1st accused.

It is alleged that the petitioners subjected the complainant

to physical and mental cruelty demanding more dowry. It is

stated that the 1st accused demanded Rs.3 lakhs for

arranging visa to go abroad and when it was not paid, she

was subjected to cruelty. It is further contended that when

the complainant was sleeping in her room at mid night, the

petitioner entered the room and attempted to outrage her

modesty by touching her body.

     3.    The learned counsel for the petitioner submits that

the allegations are not true.

     4.    Considering all the aspects the following directions

are issued:

     The petitioner shall surrender before the Investigating

Officer within ten days from today. After interrogation the

accused shall be produced before the learned Magistrate.

When applied for bail by the accused, the learned Magistrate

will, considering the nature of the case, grant bail to the

petitioner but on the following conditions:

              in case the Magistrate has any doubt

              about the genuineness or correctness of

              the tax receipts produced by the

              sureties, the learned Magistrate can

              insist for production of the attested
