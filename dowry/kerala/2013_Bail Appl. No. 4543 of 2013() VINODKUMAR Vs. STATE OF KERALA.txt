                                  O R D E R

      Petitioner is accused in crime No.843/2013 of the Paripally

Police Station originally registered for offences punishable under

sections 323, 324, 294(b), 506(1), 498 A of IPC and as learned

Public Prosecutor submits, the accusation now is only under

section 498 A IPC.       Petitioner apprehends arrest and seeks bail.

      2. Petitioner married the defacto complainant on 12.7.1997.

It is alleged that demanding Rs.5 Lakhs as dowry, he subjected

the defacto complainant to cruelty.

      3. Heard the learned Public Prosecutor also.

      4. Having regard to the nature of allegations I am inclined

to grant relief to the petitioner.

      Resultantly, this petition is allowed as under:

         1.  It is directed that in case the petitioner                is

            arrested in connection with crime No.846/2013 of

            the Paripally Police Station), he shall be released

            on bail on his executing bond for Rs. 20,000/-

            (Rupees Twenty thousand only) with two sureties

            for the like sum each before the arresting officer.

       3.   Petitioner shall not influence the        witnesses or
