                               ORDER


      Petitioner is the first accused in Crime No.209 of 2012 of

Velloor Police Station, Kottayam District. The offence alleged

against him and other accused is under Sections 498(A) r/w

Section 34 of I.P.C. and also under Sections 66(E), 67, 67(A) of

Information Technology Act and also under Section 4 of Dowry

Prohibition Act.

      2.    The allegation is that the complainant was married

by the first accused on 08/05/2011. The complainant and first

accused stayed together for about one month. Thereafter, he

left for Gulf.   A complaint was filed by her before N.S.S.

Karayogam on 20/01/2012. As the petitioner got employment

in K.S.F.E. he came from Gulf on 02/05/2012 and joined the

K.S.F.E. on 14/05/2012. The allegation against the petitioner

is that he had taken nude photos of the complainant and it was

shown to others. Learned counsel for the petitioner submits

that there was no allegation that he transmitted such photos
