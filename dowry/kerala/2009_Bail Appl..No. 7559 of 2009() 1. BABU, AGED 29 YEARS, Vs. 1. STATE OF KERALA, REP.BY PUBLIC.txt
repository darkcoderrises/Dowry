                                 O R D E R


        This is an application for bail under Section 439 of the Code of

Criminal Procedure. The petitioner is the accused in Crime No.548

of 2009 of Sulthan Bathery Police Station.



        2.  The offences alleged against the petitioner are under

Sections 498A, 306 and 304B of the Indian Penal Code.



        3. The wife of the petitioner committed suicide on 26.11.2009.

It is alleged that she committed suicide due to the cruelty meted out

by her husband (petitioner).         It is also alleged that her husband

(petitioner) is an alcoholic and he used to physically and mentally

harass her demanding more dowry. The petitioner was arrested on

28.11.2009. He was remanded to judicial custody on 29.11.2009.



        4.  Heard the learned counsel for the petitioner and the

learned Public Prosecutor. I have also perused the case diary and

the statement made by the doctor to the investigating officer.

B.A. NO. 7559 OF 2009

                                       :: 2 ::

      5.  Taking into account the facts and circumstances of the

case, the duration of the judicial custody undergone by the petitioner,

the nature of the offence and the present stage of investigation, I am

of the view that bail can be granted to the petitioner.



      6. The petitioner shall be released on bail on his executing

bond for Rs.15,000/- with two solvent sureties for the like amount to

the satisfaction of the Judicial Magistrate of the First       Class -I,

Sulthan Bathery, subject to the following conditions:

       a)    The petitioner shall report before the investigating officer
             between 9 A.M.        and 11 A.M. on all Mondays, till the
             final report is filed or until further orders;

       b)    The petitioner shall appear before the investigating
             officer for interrogation as and when required;

       c)    The petitioner shall not try to influence the prosecution
             witnesses or tamper with the evidence;

       d)    The petitioner shall not commit any offence or indulge in
             any prejudicial activity while on bail;

       e)    In case of breach of any of the conditions mentioned
             above, the bail shall be liable to be cancelled.

       The Bail Application is allowed as above.
