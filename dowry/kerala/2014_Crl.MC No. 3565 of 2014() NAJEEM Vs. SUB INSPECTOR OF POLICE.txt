                           O R D E R

     Petitioners are accused in Crime No.520/2014 of

Chavara Police Station, which has been registered for

offences under sections 323 and 498A r/w 34 of IPC.

     2.   The 1st petitioner is the husband of the 1st

informant and other petitioners are his relatives.         It is

alleged that, demanding more dowry, the petitioners

subjected the 1st informant to cruelty.     It is submitted that

the matter has been settled between parties and the

criminal case may be quashed.

     3.   Heard the learned counsel for the petitioners, 2nd

respondent and the learned Public Prosecutor.

     4.   The 1st petitioner is the husband of the 1st

informant.   Other petitioners are his relatives.       The 2nd

respondent, who is the 1st informant, has entered

Crl.M.C. No. 3565/2014
                             -2-



appearance through a counsel and filed affidavit to the

effect that the dispute has been settled between parties and

they propose to live together and the criminal case need not

be proceeded with.

      5.    I am satisfied that the ground is sufficient to

quash the proceedings in the criminal case.

      In the result, this Crl.M.C. is allowed and the

proceedings in Crime No.520/2014 of Chavara Police Station

are quashed.
