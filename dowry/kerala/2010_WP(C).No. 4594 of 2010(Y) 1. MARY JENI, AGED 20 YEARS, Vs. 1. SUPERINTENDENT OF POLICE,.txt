                          J U D G M E N T

K.M.Joseph, J.

                This writ petition is filed seeking the following

reliefs:

     "i).to issue a writ of mandamus or any other
     appropriate writ order or direction directing the Ist
     and 2nd respondents to provide adequate police
     protection to the life of the petitioner and her family
     members from the threat of the 3rd respondent.

     ii)  to issue a writ of mandamus or any other writ
     order    or   direction   directing   the   Ist   and   2nd
     respondents to take appropriate steps in Exhibit P1
     complaint."

             2. Briefly put, the case of the petitioner is as

follows:--Petitioner is residing with her mother. Petitioner is

the wife of the 3rd respondent.            The 3rd respondent is

employed in Mumbai. It is stated that after one month from

the date of marriage, the 3rd respondent returned to Mumbai

for his business. It is further stated that after about one

month the 3rd respondent came back to his home and that the

behaviour of the 3rd respondent is changed. He is consuming

alcohol and started harassing the petitioner both physically

and mentally and demanded more dowry.                 Petitioner was

forced to leave the house of the 3rd respondent and started

living with her mother, it is submitted. There is allegation of

forceful trespass and threat to kill by the 3rd respondent.

            3. A counter affidavit has been filed denying the

material allegations in the writ petition.

              4. Though the learned counsel for the petitioner

sought time to file reply affidavit, we heard the learned

counsel for the petitioner and 3rd respondent. The counsel for

the 3rd respondent would submit that the 3rd respondent has

no intention to cause any threat.

            Recording the     above submission of the learned

counsel for the 3rd respondent we dispose of the writ petition.

We direct that as and when required            by the petitioner,

protection will be afforded by respondents 1 and 2 to the life

of the petitioner, her mother and her child from any threat by

the 3rd respondent.

            The writ petition is disposed of as above.
