                               O R D E R


      Petitioners, who are the accused in F.I.R. No. 345 of 2010 of

Women Police Station, south Zozne, Hyderabad, for offences

punishable under Sections 498 A and 406 of I.P.C. and Sections 4

and 6 of Dowry Prohibition Act, seeks anticipatory bail.

     2.     Eventhough with regard to a case pending before a court

located beyond the territorial limits of State of Kerala, this Court has

no jurisdiction to grant anticipatory bail unless the petitioners are

sought to be arrested within the limits of State of Kerala, in view of

the decision Madhusoodan v. Superintendent of Police. (1992(2)

KLT 83).      The petitioners are accordingly directed to surrender

before the    Court of the Metropolitan Magistrate-15, Hyderabad,

and execute a bond for `15,000/- (Rupees fifteen thousand only) with

two solvent sureties each for the like amount to the satisfaction of the
