                          O R D E R


     Petitioners are the accused in Crime No.851/2012 of

Kayamkulam Police Station, Alappuzha District.             The

offence alleged against them is under Sec.498A r/w 34 IPC.

Apprehending arrest, this petition is filed for anticipatory

bail.

     2.   First petitioner is the husband of the complainant.

2nd petitioner is the mother of the first petitioner.      It is

alleged that petitioners subjected the complainant to

physical and mental cruelty demanding more dowry etc.

     3.   Learned counsel for the petitioners submits that

the allegations are not true.

     4.   Considering     all   the    aspects,   the following

directions are issued :-

          In the event of arrest, the petitioners shall be
