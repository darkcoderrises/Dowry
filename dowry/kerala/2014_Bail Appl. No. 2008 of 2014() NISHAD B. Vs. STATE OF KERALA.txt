                            O R D E R

     The petitioner is the accused in Crime No.389/2014 of

Kayamkulam Police Station, Alappuzha District. The offence

alleged is under section 498A of IPC. Apprehending arrest,

this petition is filed for anticipatory bail.

     2.    The defacto complainant is the wife of the

petitioner.   It is alleged that the petitioner subjected the

defacto    complainant     to physical     and    mental cruelty

demanding more money, dowry etc.

     3.    It is submitted by the learned Public Prosecutor

that the car belonging to the complainant's father was taken

away by the petitioner herein.        Subsequently, as per the

oral direction of this Court, the vehicle, insurance papers etc

were handed over to the complainant's father. It is stated

that the S.S.L.C. Book and mobile phone of the complainant
