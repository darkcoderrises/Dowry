                           ORDER

     Petitioner is the accused in Crime No.33/2014 of

Nooranadu police station, Alappuzha district. The offence

alleged against the accused is under Sec.498A IPC.

     2.   The complaint is filed by the wife of the petitioner

stating that she was subjected to physical and mental

cruelty demanding more dowry. The learned counsel for

the petitioner submits that the allegations are not true.

     3.   Considering    all  the    aspects   the   following

directions are issued:

     The petitioner shall surrender before the Investigating

Officer within two weeks from today. After interrogation

the accused shall be produced before the learned

Magistrate.   When applied for bail by the accused, the
