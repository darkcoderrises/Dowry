                         O R D E R


     The petitioners are accused in Crime No.62 of

2013 of Vadakkekara Police Station, Ernakulam

District. Prosecution alleges commission of offences

punishable under Sections 498(A), 109 r/w Section

34 of the Indian Penal Code. The allegation is that

the accused has harassed the defacto complainant

demanding more dowry and gold ornaments.

     2. Having regard to the facts and circumstances

involved in the matter, I am of the view that

custodial interrogation is not required in the case.

     In the result, this application is allowed as

follows:

     (i)  The petitioners shall appear before the

Investigating Officer at 10 a.m. on every Monday for

    (ii)    In the event of the petitioners being

arrested they shall be released on bail on executing

Thousand only) each with two solvent sureties for

the like sum.
