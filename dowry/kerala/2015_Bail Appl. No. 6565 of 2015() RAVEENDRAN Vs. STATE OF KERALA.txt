                             ORDER


          Bail application filed under Sec.438 Cr.P.C

          2. The petitioners are the father in law and

mother in law respectively of the de facto complainant,

who are involved in crime No.1165/2015 of Valappad

Police Station registered under Sections 498(A) of IPC and

Section 3 and 4 of the Dowry Prohibition Act.

          3. Heard both the sides.

          4. The prosecution case is that, the petitioners

treated the de facto complainant cruelly and harassed her

for dowry.

          5.    Considering the nature of allegations, pre

arrest bail is granted with the following conditions.

            1. In the event of arrest, the petitioners

     shall be released on their executing a bond for

     Rs.25,000/- (Rupees twenty five thousand only)

     each with two solvent sureties each for the like

     sum to the satisfaction of the investigating

Bail Appl. No.6565 of 2015         2

       officer.

             3. The petitioners shall appear before the

      Investigating Officer as and when directed.

             4. The petitioners shall not intimidate or

      attempt to influence the witnesses, nor shall

      they tamper with the evidence.

      5.    The     petitioner   shall   co-operate with

      investigation of the case.

             In case of violation of any of the above

conditions, the learned Magistrate is empowered to cancel

the bail in accordance with law without referring the

matter to this Court.
