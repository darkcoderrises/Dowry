                               O R D E R


      Petitioner is the accused in Crime No.781/2014 of

Kottarakkara Police Station. The offence alleged against him is

punishable under Sec.498A of IPC. Apprehending arrest, this

petition is filed for anticipatory bail.

      2.    Petitioner is the husband of the de facto complainant.

Their marriage took place on 2.5.2007. It is alleged that the

petitioner subjected the de facto complainant to cruelty by

demanding 2 lakhs more as dowry.

      3.    Learned counsel for the petitioner submits that the

allegations are not true.

      4.    Considering all the aspects, the following directions

are issued :-

            The petitioner shall surrender before the Investigating

Officer within 10 days from today.              After interrogation the

petitioner shall be produced before the learned Magistrate.

When applied for bail by him, the learned Magistrate will grant
