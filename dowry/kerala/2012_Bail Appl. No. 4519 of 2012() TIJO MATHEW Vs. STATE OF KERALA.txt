                            O R D E R


     Petitioner is the accused in Crime No.92/2012 of

Peringom Police Station. The offences alleged against him

are under Secs.498A, 323, 324 and 506(i) IPC. Petitioner is

the husband of the complainant. Apprehending arrest, this

petition is filed for anticipatory bail.

     2.   It is alleged that the petitioner subjected his wife

to physical and mental cruelty demanding dowry and other

valuable properties.      The prosecution also contends that

there is allegation that the petitioner hit the complainant on

her abdomen with a stick and caused hurt to her.

     3.   Learned counsel for the petitioner submits that no

serious injury was caused and that the allegation was made

only to bolster up the case of the complainant.             A

conditional order was earlier passed by the learned

     4.    Considering     all  the   aspects, the following

directions are issued :-

           The     petitioner shall   surrender  before the

Investigating Officer within 10 days from today.       After

interrogation the accused shall be produced before the

learned Magistrate. When applied for bail by the accused,

the learned Magistrate can, considering the nature of the

case, grant bail to the petitioner but on the following

conditions:

           a.     The accused shall execute a bond for

Rs.50,000/- (Rupees Fifty thousand only) with two solvent

sureties each for the like sum to the satisfaction of the

learned Magistrate. If in case the Magistrate has any doubt

about the genuineness or correctness of the tax receipts

produced by the sureties, the learned Magistrate can insist

for production of the attested photo copies of the original
