                              ORDER

      The petitioners are accused in Crime No.396/2012 of

Kothamangalam Police Station, Ernakulam District. The offences

alleged are under sections 498(A), 294(b) and 34 of IPC.

Apprehending arrest, this petition is filed for anticipatory bail.




      2.   The first petitioner is the husband of the defacto

complainant.    The second petitioner is the mother of the first

petitioner. The marriage between the defacto complainant and

the first accused took place on 24-1-2012. The allegation is that

the defacto complainant was subjected to physical and mental

cruelty demanding more money (dowry) by the first and second

petitioners.




      Considering the circumstances mentioned above, the

following directions are issued :-

      In the event of arrest, the petitioners shall be released on
