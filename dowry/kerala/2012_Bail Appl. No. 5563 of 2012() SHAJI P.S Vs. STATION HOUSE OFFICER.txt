                           ORDER

     Petitioners are the accused in Crime No.739/2012 of

Valapattanam Police Station, Kannur District. The offence

alleged against them is under Sec.498A of IPC.

Apprehending arrest, this petition is filed for anticipatory

bail.

     2.    First   petitioner   is the   husband    of   the

complainant. Second petitioner is the mother of the first

petitioner and the third petitioner is stated to be the wife

of the first petitioner's uncle.

     3.    Learned counsel for the petitioners submits

that the allegations of cruelty are not true and similarly

allegation of demand of dowry is also not correct.

     4.    Considering all the aspects, the following

directions are issued:

     5.     The first petitioner shall surrender before the

Investigating Officer within two weeks from today. After

interrogation the accused shall be produced before the

learned Magistrate. When applied for bail by the accused,

the learned Magistrate will, considering the nature of the

case, grant bail to the first petitioner but on the following

conditions:

         a. The accused shall execute a bond for

              Rs.25,000/- (Rupees twenty five thousand

              only) with two solvent sureties each for

              the like sum to the satisfaction of the

              concerned     Court.    If   in  case    the

              Magistrate has any doubt about the

              genuineness or correctness of the tax

              receipts produced by the sureties, the

              learned   Magistrate      can    insist  for

              production of the attested photo copies of

              the original title deeds of the sureties.


         b. The accused shall make himself available

              for interrogation by the Investigating

              Officer and that he should appear before

              the Investigating Officer on all Mondays

      In the event of arrest, the second and third

petitioners shall be released on bail on their executing a

bond for Rs.20,000/- (Rupees Twenty Thousand only)

If not arrested, the second and third petitioners will

surrender      before    the    learned   Magistrate   having

regular bail. The learned Magistrate will grant bail to the
