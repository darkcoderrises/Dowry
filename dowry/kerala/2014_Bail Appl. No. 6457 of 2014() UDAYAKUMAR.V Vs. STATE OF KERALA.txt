                               ORDER


            Petitioner is the first accused in Crime No.409/2014 of

Neyyar Dam Police Station. Apprehending arrest in the above crime,

he preffered the present application for anticipatory bail under Section

438 of the Code of Criminal Procedure.

      2. The prosecution allegation is that the marriage between the

petitioner and the de facto complainant was solomnized on 08.09.2002

and while the de facto complainant was residing with the 1st accused,

she was subjected to cruelty demanding more dowry. It is also the

allegation of the prosecution that the 1st and 2nd accused were in love

and after the marriage of the 1st accused with the de facto complainant

the 1st and 2nd accused harassed the de facto complainant mentally. So,

according to the prosecution, the accused have committed the offences

punishable under Section 498A r/w Section 34 of the Indian Penal

Code.

       4.     The learned counsel for the petitioner submitted that the

petitioner is implicated in the above crime as part of matrimonial

       5.     On getting instructions, the learned Public Prosecutor

submitted that the allegations are general in nature and no specific

       6. Having regard to the facts and circumstances involved in the

       In the result, this petition is allowed and, accordingly, there will
