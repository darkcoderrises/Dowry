                               Order

      The petitioner is the accused in Crime No.885 of 2012 of

Koratty Police Station. He is the husband of the complainant. The

offence alleged against him is under Section 498A IPC. They are

blessed with two children. The allegation against the petitioner is

that he mentally and physically harassed the complainant

demanding more dowry. The complainant preferred a private

complaint (Annexure A) before JFCM Court, Chalakudy and the

same was forwarded for investigation and on that basis the

present crime was registered. The learned counsel for the

petitioner submits that the petitioner is innocent.

      2. Considering all the aspects, the following directions are

issued:

      In the event of arrest, the petitioner shall be released on

bail on his executing a bond for Rs.20,000/- (Rupees Twenty

Thousand only) each with two solvent sureties each for the like
