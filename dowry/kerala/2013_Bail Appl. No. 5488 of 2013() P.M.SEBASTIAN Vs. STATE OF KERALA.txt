                            O R D E R

      Petitioners are two among the accused (A2 and A3) in

crime No. 545/2013 of Ottappalam Police Station registered

for the offences punishable under Sections 302 and 304 (B)

read with Section 34 Indian Penal Code. They have filed the

above application seeking their release on bail under Section

439 of Cr.P.C.

      2. 1st accused in the crime is the son of petitioners. Wife

of 1st accused namely Rima, after murdering her one and half

year old child born out of the wedlock with 1st accused,

committed suicide on 28.03.2013 at the matrimonial home.

Over the above occurrence, crime was registered against her

husband and his parents for the offences stated supra.

Investigation of that crime is still in progress. Allegation is

that wife of 1st accused, Rima, committed suicide, after

murdering her infant child, unable to bear the cruelties inflicted

by the accused persons demanding dowry.

      3. Learned counsel for petitioners submits that the 1st

accused had already been enlarged on bail by Orders passed

B.A. No.5488/2013               2


by this Court in his application B.A.No. 3321/2013, subject to

the conditions imposed thereunder.       Petitioners after their

arrest on 14.07.2013 continues in custody ever since and their

further detention is not essential for a fair and proper

investigation     of crime, is the submission of counsel.

Petitioners are innocent and are prepared to abide by any

condition imposed by this Court, is the submission of counsel

to urge for their release on bail.     In case petitioners are

released, adequate conditions be imposed to safeguard the

investigation of crime, is the submission of learned Public

Prosecutor.

        4. Considering the submissions made as above and

taking note of the period of detention already suffered by

petitioners, they are directed to be released on bail subject to

the following conditions.

i. Petitioners shall execute bond for Rs.25,000/- (Rupees

Twenty Five Thousand only) with two solvent sureties for the

like sum by each of them to the satisfaction of Judicial First

Class Magistrate Court, Ottappalam.

ii. Petitioners shall report before the investigating officer as

and when directed till completion of investigation.

B.A. No.5488/2013                 3


iii.Petitioners shall not leave the State for a period of six

   months or till completion of investigation of crime, whichever

   is earlier, without getting prior permission from the

   investigating officer in writing.

iv.Petitioners shall not intimidate, terrorize or influence any

   person acquainted with the facts of the case so as to

   dissuade him from disclosing such facts before the police or

   court, as the case may be, and they shall not do any act to

   tamper with or make any attempt to obliterate the evidence

   of crime.
