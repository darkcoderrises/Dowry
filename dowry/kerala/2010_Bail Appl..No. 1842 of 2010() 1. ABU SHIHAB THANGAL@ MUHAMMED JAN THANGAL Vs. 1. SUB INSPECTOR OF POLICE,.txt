                                ORDER


     The petitioner, who is the second accused in Crime

No.124 of 2010 of Feroke Police Station for offences

punishable under Sections 498-A and 406 read with Section

34 I.P.C. and Section 4 of the Dowry Prohibition Act, 1961,

seeks anticipatory bail.

     2.     Pending this application the marital parties have

settled the matter in the Lok Adalat under the High Court

Legal Services Committee. The F.I.R. itself has been quashed

by this Court in Crl.M.C.No.3835 of 2010. If so, the petitioner

cannot have any reasonable apprehension of arrest and

harassment by the police.

     Recording the settlement arrived at between the parties

this petition is closed.



                                            V.RAMKUMAR, JUDGE
