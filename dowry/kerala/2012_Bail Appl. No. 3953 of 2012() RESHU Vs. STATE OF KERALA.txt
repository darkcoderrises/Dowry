                              O R D E R

     Petitioners    are accused Nos. 1 to 4 in Crime

No.191/2012         of        Anjuthengu            Police     Station,

Thiruvananthapuram District. The offences alleged against

them are under sections 498A and 323 r/w 34 of IPC.

Apprehending arrest, this petition is filed for anticipatory

bail.

     2.   The     first   accused       is   the    husband    of  the

complainant. Petitioners 2 and 3 are the father and mother

of A1. The fourth petitioner is the sister of A1. It is alleged

that the fourth petitioner hacked on the face of the

complainant. It is also alleged that the petitioners subjected

the complainant to physical and mental cruelty and also

made illegal demand for dowry.                     According to the

petitioners, the allegations are false.

     3.   Considering all the circumstances the following

B.A.No.3953/2012                -2-

directions are issued :-

     In the event of arrest, petitioners shall be released on

bail on their executing a bond for Rs.20,000/- (Rupees

Twenty Thousand only) each with two solvent sureties each

for the like amount to the satisfaction of the officer obtaining

the bond as above. If not arrested, petitioners will surrender

before the learned Magistrate having jurisdiction within 10

days from this date and will seek regular bail. The learned

Magistrate will grant bail to the accused on their executing a

bond for Rs.20,000/- and on such other conditions the

learned Magistrate may deem fit to impose, if necessary.
