                         ORDER


     Petitioner is the 2nd accused in Crime No.604/2012

of Alathur Police Station, Palakkad District. The offences

alleged against him are under Secs.498A and 304B r/w 34

of IPC. Apprehending arrest, this petition is filed for

anticipatory bail.

     2.   The deceased Silpa was married by first

accused. It was a love marriage. Subsequent to the

marriage, this petitioner demanded Rs.50,000/- and gold

ornaments to be brought by the deceased from her

parental house as dowry. First accused was told by the

parents of the deceased about their inability. First

accused told that the direction given by this petitioner

has to be complied with. Parents sought one year time for

payment of money and gold ornaments. It is alleged that

the first accused and this petitioner subjected the

deceased to physical and mental cruelty demanding

dowry etc.

     3.     Learned counsel for the petitioner submits that

the first accused was arrested and he was in custody for

some     time.   The    deceased    committed     suicide by

consuming leaves of a poisonous plant. Postmortem

certificate also agrees with that finding.

     4.     Considering all the aspects, the following

directions are issued:

     5.     The petitioner shall surrender before the

Investigating Officer within 10 days from today. After

interrogation the accused shall be produced before the

learned Magistrate. When applied for bail by the accused,

the learned Magistrate can, considering the nature of the

case, grant bail to the petitioner but on the following

conditions:

         a. The accused shall execute a bond for

              Rs.25,000/- (Rupees twenty five thousand
