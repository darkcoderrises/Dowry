                                 O R D E R

      The first petitioner is the husband of the third respondent.

The second petitioner is his mother, the third petitioner his cousin

and the fourth petitioner the sister of the second petitioner.

      2. The petitioners are accused of having committed the

offences under sections 323, 377 and 498A IPC, Section 4 of the

Dowry Prohibition Act and 66C of the Information Technology Act. It

is submitted that this matter along with the other disputes has been

settled and the proceedings in the criminal case may be quashed.

      3. Heard the learned counsel for the petitioner and the third

respondent and the learned Public Prosecutor.

      4. The third respondent has filed an affidavit to the effect that

all the disputes between the parties concerned have been settled

and she has no objection to the proceedings in the criminal case

being quashed. I am satisfied that the allegations in the petition are

true and no public interest is involved in this case.

      In the result, the petition is allowed proceedings in Crime No.

1358 of 2013 of Sreekariyam Police Station are quashed.
