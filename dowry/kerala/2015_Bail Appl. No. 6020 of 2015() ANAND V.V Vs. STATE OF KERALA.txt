                                      ORDER



             Petition under Section 438 of the Code of Criminal Procedure.


       2.    Petitioners  are     the     husband         and   in-laws of defacto

complainant. They are involved in Crime No.288 of 2015 of Chombala

Police Station registered for offences punishable under Sections 498A, 406

and 506(ii) read with Section 34 of the Indian Penal Code.

       3.    Marriage between the defacto complainant and the first

petitioner was on 05.05.2011. It is alleged that after sometime a cleavage

in the relationship appeared and the relationship became strained. It is

further alleged that the defacto complainant was harassed demanding

dowry.

       4.    Heard both sides.

             Considering the nature of allegations and the relationship

between the parties, following order is passed:

             i.    In the event of arrest in Crime No.288 of 2015 of

Chombala Police Station, the petitioners shall be released on bail on each

one of them executing a bond for `25,000/- (Rupees twentyfive thousand

BA No.6020/2015                         2

only) with two solvent sureties each for the like sum to the satisfaction of

the investigating officer.

               ii.  The sureties shall produce documents to establish their

identity and solvency.

               iii. Petitioners shall appear before the Investigating Officer

as and when directed in writing and co-operate with the investigation in the

matter.

               iv.  They shall not influence or intimidate the witnesses.

               In case of violation of any of the above conditions, the officer

concerned is free to arrest the petitioner as if no order is passed.
