                             O R D E R

      The petitioner is the sole accused in Crime No.1150/2012

of Vadakkencherry Police Station for having committed the

offence punishable under Section 498A of the IPC.                   The

petitioner married the defacto complainant on 7.1.1996 and

there are two children in the wedlock. A complaint was laid on

19.7.2012 alleging that the petitioner demanded more dowry

from the defacto complainant.

      2.   Considering the nature of allegation made against the

petitioner and nature of offences committed, it is felt that it is a

fit case when the jurisdiction under Section 438 of Cr.P.C need

to be exercised in favour of the petitioner.

      3.  The petition is accordingly allowed on the following

conditions:

         i)   The petitioner shall surrender before the JFCM,
    Alathur on or before 19.9.2012 and move an application
    for regular bail which the learned Magistrate shall allow
    on the petitioner executing a bond for a sum of
    Rs.15,000/- (Rupees fifteen thousand only) with two
    solvent sureties each for the like sum to the satisfaction of
    the said Court.
