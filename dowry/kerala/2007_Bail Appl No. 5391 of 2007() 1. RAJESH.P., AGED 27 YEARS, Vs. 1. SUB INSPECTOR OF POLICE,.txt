      Application for regular bail. The petitioner is the husband of the

deceased.    She died within seven years of the marriage. It was

allegedly a love marriage.     Wife committed suicide while she was

residing in the marital home. It is alleged that the husband was guilty

of matrimonial cruelty of the culpable variety.     There is no specific

allegation of any demand for dowry.         The arrest took place on

17.08.07. He continues in custody from that date.

      2. The learned counsel for the petitioner prays, the learned

Public Prosecutor does not oppose the said prayer on condition

that the Investigators are granted reasonable further time to

complete the investigation and I am satisfied that regular bail can

be granted to the petitioner subject to appropriate conditions.

      3.    In the result, this petition is allowed. The petitioner

shall be released on bail on the following terms and conditions:

      i)    The petitioner shall not be released from custody on

the strength of this order prior to 15.09.2007.The Investigators

shall, in the meantime, make every endeavour to complete the

      ii)  He shall execute a bond for Rs. 1,00,000/- (Rupees

One lakh only) with two solvent sureties each for the like sum to

the satisfaction of the learned Magistrate.

      iii) The petitioner shall make himself available for

interrogation before the investigating officer  between 10 a.m

and 12 noon on all Mondays and Fridays for a period of thre

months and thereafter as and when directed by the investigating

officer in writing to do so.
