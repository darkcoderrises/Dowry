                             ORDER


        Apprehending arrest in Crime No. 165/2014 of Pattambi

Police Station, petitioners who are accused numbers 2, 3 and 5 to 9

preferred the above application for anticipatory bail under Section

438 of the Code of Criminal Procedure.

     2. The prosecution allegation is that the marriage between

the de facto complainant and the 1st accused was solomnized on

28.07.2011 as per customary rites of Muslims and the de facto

complainant was subjected to harassment, both physically and

mentally, demanding more dowry and thus, the accused, 9 in

numbers have committed the offences punishable under Section

498A of the Indian Penal Code.

B.A No.5932/2014                    2



       3. Heard the learned counsel for the petitioners as well as the

learned Public Prosecutor.

       4. The learned counsel for the petitioners submitted that the

present crime was registered connected with a matrimonial dispute

and the petitioners are no way connected with the offence alleged

against them.

       5. The learned Public Prosecutor, on instructions, submitted

that the allegation against the accused are general in nature and no

specific overt act is alleged against the petitioners.

       6. Having regard to the facts and circumstances involved in

the case and considering the submissions made by the counsel, I

am of the view that this petition can be allowed, but, subject to

conditions.

       In the result, this petition is allowed and, accordingly, there

will be a direction that in the event of the arrest of the petitioners in

Crime No.165/2014 of Pattambi Police Station, they shall be

released on bail on each of them executing a bond for `35,000/-

(Rupees Thirty Five Thousand only) with two solvent sureties,

          i)    Petitioners 3, 4 and 6 are directed to report
