          Petition under Section 438 Cr.P.C.

     2.   Petitioner        is    the      3rd    accused         in  Crime

No.1641/2014 of Paravur Police Station, Kollam district

pending investigation for the offences punishable under

Sections 498A and 306 read with Section 34 IPC.

     3.   Deceased Sunisha was given in marriage to the

1st accused. After the marriage, she was residing at the

matrimonial house along with the 1st accused, his mother

the 2nd accused and his younger brother the 3rd accused.

She was being harassed and tortured to the maximum.

Every now and then she was being beaten up severely by

demanding more dowry. Finally, the girl had no other go

than to commit suicide by jumping in front of a running train

on 18.09.2014 at the railway track at a place called

Kuzhikarathazham.

      4.    Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and

learned Public Prosecutor.

      5.    The contents of the CD, prima facie, reveal the

complicity of the petitioner. There were tortures and

harassments from the part of the petitioner also. The

investigation has revealed that the 1st accused had gone

abroad on the next morning of the death of the deceased.

It has further come out that the present petitioner is

indulging in threatening and intimidating all the neighbours

from giving any information or furnishing any statement

before the police regarding the true state of affairs

happened at the house on the dates prior to the incident.

Much discussion is not required to conclude that the

petitioner is not entitled to the discretionary relief of

anticipatory bail.

      In the result, this bail application is dismissed.
